syllabus

A syllabus provides a structured roadmap for learning, outlining objectives, topics, and expectations. It guides time management, ensures consistency, fosters accountability, and supports self-directed learning. Serving as a contract between students and educators, it helps prepare for exams, facilitates communication, and ensures comprehensive curriculum coverage and evaluation.

PYQS

Previous Year Question Papers (PYQs) help understand exam patterns, highlight important topics, and reveal frequently asked questions. They improve time management, boost confidence, and reduce exam anxiety. Practicing PYQs helps assess preparation levels, identify strengths and weaknesses, and refine strategies for better performance in exams.
